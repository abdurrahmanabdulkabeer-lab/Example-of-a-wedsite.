About Us

Masjid Ibadulrahman is located in Kwalakota, Minna, Niger State. We serve the local community with spiritual guidance, Quranic tafsir sessions, an Islamic library of books, and Islamiyya education for children and adults.

Our Services

Tafsir

Weekly tafsir classes and study circles led by our local scholars. Open to everyone.

Books & Library

Islamic books available for study and purchase — from basics to advanced texts in Arabic and English.

Islamiyya

Islamiyya classes for children and adults: Quran reading, tajweed and basic Islamic knowledge.

Featured Books

We stock Tafsir, Hadith collections, Seerah, and beginner books for kids. Visit the masjid to browse the collection.

Islamiyya Program

Structured classes for children and adults focusing on Quran reading, memorization, tajweed, and Islamic manners. Contact us to enroll or volunteer as a teacher.
